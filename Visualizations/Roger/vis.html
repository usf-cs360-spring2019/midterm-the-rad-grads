Roger's Visualization

Interpretation

This visualization is about deifferent call type percentage between 2018-2019. Medical incident call have the most part of call. Since medical incident have 70.3%, it's hard to compare the smallest 5 call type. In this case, you can remove any kind of call type by click on legend. For example, if you click medical incident in legend, you will get other call type percentage beside medical incident. And the information show on pie chart when mouse move on, the percentage is recalculated.

Discussion

The primary goal of this visualization was show which kind of call type have the biggest number between 2018-2019 and the distribution of call type.
